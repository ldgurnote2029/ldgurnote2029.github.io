ABOUT K-POP

WHAT IS K-POP?

K-pop, abbreviated term for popular music/genre from South Korea (Korean Pop). K-pop is typically used to describe “idol music” made by artists trained by entertainment companies to become pop music stars. K-pop includes upbeat pop, dance music, and ballads. It can incorporate a variety of influences, including reggae, hip-hop, salsa, and other types of music (Colón & Suzan, 2025).
